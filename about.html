Triple J Catering, owned and run by Susan Stewart, is based out of the Sacramento area of Northern California. Susan has been catering for over 25 years and enjoys nothing more than using her food and decorations to create amazing events. She caters all sort of events including: weddings, receptions, church celebrations and more. In addition to her cooking, her stylish decorations are sure to blow you away. If you are looking to turn your event into a memory to last a lifetime-look no further! With Susan's vast years of experience and expertease Triple J Catering is sure to meet your every need and desire.
